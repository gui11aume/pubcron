Moar Personal

PubCron is a personal PubMed watch. With over 2,000 references on PubMed every day, PubCron helps you follow the information that you find relevant. PubCron sends emails containing a personal selection of PubMed abstracts.

Moar and moar personal

PubCron learns what you like. You can tell PubCron which abstracts you find relevant, and which you'd rather not read. In a couple of days, PubCron will adapt and send you a more personal selection.
